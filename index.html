Campus Life

school ground

The campus boasts of a large sporting facility strengthened by a structured sports program. The school grounds and sports program provide students with an opportunity to hone their skills in Team (Cricket, Football) as well as individual sports (Badminton, Table Tennis, Skating, Archery, Karate, Taekwondo, Chess) and athletics. School sports infrastructure include basketball courts, badminton courts, football field, cricket pitch, skating ring, volleyball courts and an athletics field.

school library

The school has two libraries, one in the Montessori Primary and one in the Senior school, stocked with over 4 Lakh titles that cover fiction and non-fiction in all genres and subjects. In addition, the library contains a stock of multimedia materials for use in the classes. KNMS believes in inculcating a strong reading habit in its children. Students are actively encouraged to use the library for independent study and for research towards their presentations and projects.

school's biology lab

School labs are a great place for students which help them enhance their learning by understanding the theoretical concepts of science which are taught in classrooms. Well-designed laboratories not only make science experiments fun but also help students in achieving good academic results.
